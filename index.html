About Me

I have more than seven years of experience in video editing using Premiere Pro, where I focus on crafting clean cuts, thoughtful transitions, and polished color grading. My time at Studio Stile strengthened my ability to lead teams, coordinate efficient workflows, and keep communication clear under tight deadlines.

Beyond the editing suite, I automate repetitive tasks with Python and C-family languages, and I am certified as a Microsoft Office Specialist. Speaking Haitian Creole helps me connect with diverse collaborators and communities.

Currently enrolled in the Disney Aspire Program and pursuing a computer science degree, I bring a mix of creativity, technical rigor, and curiosity to every project.
