About Me

I have more than seven years of experience in video editing using Premiere Pro. During this time, I have developed expertise in various areas such as trimming, merging, transitions, effects, color grading, audio synchronization, and exporting. In addition to my technical skills, I have gained valuable team management experience while working at Studio Stile. There, I effectively coordinate tasks, ensure efficient workflows, and maintain clear communication among team members. I am also proficient in programming languages such as CS, C, C++, and Python. This allows me to automate tasks and troubleshoot video editing projects effectively. Furthermore, I hold a Microsoft Office Specialist certification, demonstrating my proficiency in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. I possess multilingual proficiencies in Haitian Creole, with this I can connect with diverse audiences and work in multicultural settings. I am motivated and always eager to take on new challenges. Currently, I am participating in the Disney Aspire Program while pursuing a computer science degree. I bring a well-rounded skill set, dedication, and a strong passion for growth to any project or organization I am a part of.
